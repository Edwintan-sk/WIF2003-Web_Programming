1. Authentication & Account (Universal)
These pages handle the "General" module you are personally in charge of.
●	Login Page: A clean entry point with role-based authentication.
●	Register Page: Account creation for new users.
●	Profile View Page: Displays personal and professional details for both roles.
●	Update Profile/Settings: A dedicated form to edit info, change passwords, or deactivate accounts.
2. Manager Interface
As the overseers, managers need pages focused on creation, distribution, and verification.
●	Manager Dashboard: A high-level visual tracking interface showing overall team milestones and achievement scores.
●	KPI Management (Directory): A list view of all existing Key Performance Indicators.
●	Create/Edit KPI Form: A specialized page or modal to define new targets and goals.
●	KPI Assignment Center: A dedicated view to link specific KPIs to individual staff members.
●	Verification Inbox: A list of evidence submitted by staff that is pending review.
●	Evidence Detail View: A page to review uploaded files (PDFs, images) and approve or reject proof.
3. Staff Interface
Staff members need pages that prioritize their specific assignments and progress reporting.
●	Staff Dashboard: A personalized view focusing on individual progress towards assigned goals.
●	My Assigned KPIs: A clear list of specific targets the staff member needs to achieve.
●	Progress Update / Evidence Submission Page: A form-based page to input completion percentages and upload proof files.
4. Communication & Support (Integrated)
These features should be designed as components or standalone views accessible across the system.
●	Notifications Center: A panel or page for real-time push/email alerts regarding deadlines and approvals.
●	Feedback/Comments Interface: A sidebar or section within KPI pages for direct communication between staff and managers.
